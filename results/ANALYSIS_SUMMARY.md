## Mixing Recommendations

### Crossfade Times
- **Warm-Up Phase:** 32-48 bars (let grooves breathe)
- **Build Phase:** 16-32 bars (increase pace)
- **Peak Phase:** 8-16 bars (maximum momentum)
- **Breakdown:** Clean 4-8 bar break
- **Comeback:** 12-16 bars (controlled buildup)

### EQ Strategy by Phase
1. **Warm-Up:** Emphasize low-end (sub bass), subtle high-end
2. **Build:** Gradually increase mid-high frequencies, introduce percussion
3. **Peak:** Full spectrum, use filters for dynamic interest
4. **Breakdown:** Strip to vocals/synths, remove kick for space
5. **Comeback:** Reintroduce bass gradually, build layering

### Effects Recommendations
- **Warm-Up:** Reverb on pads, subtle delay on percussion
- **Build:** Resonant filters, sweeping automation
- **Peak:** Minimal effects (let tracks speak), occasional builds
- **Breakdown:** Heavy reverb, ping-pong delays
